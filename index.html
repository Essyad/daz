Exclusive Tours for Solo Travelers and Small Groups

Discover the world in style with our intimate, small-group tours designed for independent travelers and close-knit groups. These tours offer the perfect opportunity to forge connections with like-minded adventurers, share experiences, and enjoy the camaraderie that comes with traveling together—all while benefiting from unbeatable group rates.

What Sets Our Small-Group Tours Apart:

Guaranteed Departures: Once you book, your trip is confirmed—no cancellations, no uncertainties.
Inclusive Meals: Savor breakfast, lunch, and dinner every day of your journey. No hidden costs or surprise charges.
All-Inclusive Comfort: From transportation to expert guides and dedicated drivers, everything is included in the package.
Window Seat for Everyone: With no more than three guests per vehicle, every traveler enjoys a window seat for the best views and a spacious ride.
Charming Accommodations: Your stay includes all nights in cozy hotels or guesthouses, ensuring a comfortable rest at the end of each day.
Flexible Rooming Options: We offer twin accommodations for travelers sharing, but if you prefer your own space, a private room can be arranged for just €160, covering the entire tour duration.

Enhanced Security & Connectivity:

  1. Starlink Satellite Internet: Stay connected throughout the entire trip with fast, reliable Starlink satellite internet, included at no extra charge.
  2. Convoy Communication: Our vehicles are equipped with walkie-talkies to ensure constant communication between drivers, maintaining a safe and coordinated convoy.
  3. GPS Tracking: Every vehicle has GPS tracking, allowing our main office in Nouakchott to monitor your location at all times for added peace of mind.
  4. Power Banks: We provide power banks, so your devices stay charged and ready, no matter where the adventure takes you.

Additional Services

Iron ore train ride

Hop aboard one of the longest freight trains in the world for an unforgettable journey across the Sahara! This iconic iron ore train ride offers breathtaking views of sweeping desert landscapes,

rolling dunes, and unique geological formations. Feel the thrill of the open air as you chug through the heart of Mauritania, capturing stunning photographs and soaking in the vastness of the Sahara.

Camping in the desert

Experience the magic of the Sahara under a blanket of stars with our exclusive desert camping adventures! Set up your cozy tent amidst towering dunes and enjoy the serenity of the desert as night falls. Gather around a crackling campfire

share stories, and savor traditional meals prepared under the open sky. Wake up to the stunning sunrise casting golden hues over the landscape—an experience you’ll never forget!

Camel trekking

Embark on a thrilling camel trekking adventure through the mesmerizing dunes of Mauritania! Guided by experienced local nomads, you'll ride these gentle giants as you traverse the ever-shifting sands. Discover hidden oases, ancient caravan routes, and breathtaking viewpoints. This unique journey connects you with the rhythm of the desert and offers an authentic glimpse into the life of the Sahara's indigenous cultures.

Cultural immersion experiences

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Mauritania with our unique cultural immersion experiences! Participate in traditional music and dance sessions, try your hand at local crafts, or enjoy a cooking class where you’ll learn to prepare authentic Mauritanian dishes. Connect with local communities, share stories, and gain a deeper understanding of the rich traditions that shape this incredible country.
